Eating in Greensboro?

UNCG houses a variety of places to eat here on campus and if you are not satisfied with those options, there is countless more on Tate Street and beyond. This is your one-stop shop to find the most popular eats, as well as some of the most underrated. So get hungry, grab a couple of friends, and jump into UNCG's Eats!

Tate Street Coffee House

One of the staples of Tate Street, the Tate Street Coffee House has been serving UNCG students and Greensboro residents since 1993! The location serves both food and coffee as well as providing a relaxing work environment for everyone!
